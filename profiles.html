Meet The Family!

All of our animals go through a certification process to ensure the safety of our customers. All animals are up to date on shots and medication and have been evaluated as to what events they are most suited for. If you have any concerns about or animals you can contact us.

Lulu

Breed: Gray Tabby
Weight: 14lbs
Age: 15
Lulu is one of older cats of the group. The main events that she attends are calmer and quieter such as: offices, old homes, and small parties. She is great with people of all ages and loves being petted!

Sam

Breed: White American Short-hair
Weight: 15lbs
Age: 9
This is Sam, one of our newest cats! He loves attention and being held. He is good with both kids, other animals as well as loud or busy parties. This cool guy is sure to liven up any party that he enters.

Bruce

Breed: Bernese Mountain Dog
Weight: 50lbs
Age: 8
Bruce is our biggest dog that we offer, but don’t let that scare you off! He is one of the sweetest dogs that you will ever meet. He is gentle with kids and loves to give them kisses. He is good for just about every kind of event.

Pops

Breed: Short-hair Dachund
Weight: 20lbs
Age: 15
Pops is one of our only senior dogs in the family. But don’t let his old age effect your decision, he is still as active and fun as a puppy! He is suitable for any event but excels in quieter environments such as nursing homes.
